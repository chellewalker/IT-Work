Paintball Site Plan

Marshall Warner

WDD 130-01

Overview

Purpose

I want to create a site through which individuals can easily signup for paintball events. This website will have information on what the paintballing will entail and provide an easy system through which to signup for future events. Have you ever wanted to plan a fun activity but felt daunted by the process? Then this website will be exactually what you need. With this website, protentional users will be able to plot out their trip in an interactive system and prevent any of the confusion that self-planning might entail.

Audience

The primary targets of this website will be college students and young adults, people with the time and interest in planning such a physical activity. Groups of youngers individuals, such as Boy Scouts and similar, would also be a key demographic.
