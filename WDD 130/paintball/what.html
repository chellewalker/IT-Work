What use is this site?

People will come to this site as it allows a user not only to simply sign up for an event (though they could certainly do so), but also to determine how they could create their own paintball experience and what equipment and space they would need to rent or buy. All of this will be determinable from this site.

What is this site for?

People will come to this site to plan for an active day of paintballing. They will use this site both to see what to expect from a day of paintballing, as well as to plan and coordinate their own future experiences.

What types of activities can visitors complete?

While paintballing is the primary goal, visitors can also use this site to research any equipment needed to plan their own activity, as well as information on how to perform paintball activities in a typical format.
