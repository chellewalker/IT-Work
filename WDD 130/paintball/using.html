What information is necessary to use this site?

It is necessary to know beforehand what kind of event the visitor wishes to create. Do they want a small event with close friends? A group activity with members of a social circle? A community activity for the whole neighborhood? The desired scale, more than anything else, is needed when using this site.

Why come here instead of using another site?

While other sites provide purchasable planning, this site will also provide information on creating one's own paintballing experience, which would generate higher traffic and potentionally increasing its visiblity to those who are willing to spend for such an experience.
