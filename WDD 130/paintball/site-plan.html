Paintball Site Plan

Marshall Warner

WDD 130-01

Overview

Purpose

I want to create a site through which individuals can easily signup for paintball events. This website will have information on what the paintballing will entail and provide an easy system through which to signup for future events. Have you ever wanted to plan a fun activity but felt daunted by the process? Then this website will be exactually what you need. With this website, protentional users will be able to plot out their trip in an interactive system and prevent any of the confusion that self-planning might entail.

Audience

The primary targets of this website will be college students and young adults, people with the time and interest in planning such a physical activity. Groups of youngers individuals, such as Boy Scouts and similar, would also be a key demographic.

What is this Site?

Why will people come to this site?

People will come to this site as it allows a user not only to simply sign up for an event (though they could certainly do so), but also to determine how they could create their own paintball experience and what equipment and space they would need to rent or buy. All of this will be determinable from this site.

What questions will visitors expect this site to answer for them?

People will come to this site to plan for an active day of paintballing. They will use this site both to see what to expect from a day of paintballing, as well as to plan and coordinate their own future experiences.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on this site?

While paintballing is the primary goal, visitors can also use this site to research any equipment needed to plan their own activity, as well as information on how to perform paintball activities in a typical format.

Using this Site

What information is necessary to complete any actions this site offers?

It is necessary to know beforehand what kind of event the visitor wishes to create. Do they want a small event with close friends? A group activity with members of a social circle? A community activity for the whole neighborhood? The desired scale, more than anything else, is needed when using this site.

Why will visitors come to this site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

While other sites provide purchasable planning, this site will also provide information on creating one's own paintballing experience, which would generate higher traffic and potentionally increasing its visiblity to those who are willing to spend for such an experience.
